Architecture

How NarrativePilot works

Five autonomous modules running in a continuous loop. No manual intervention required.

01 / FETCH
CMC Agent Hub
Connects via MCP every 15 minutes. Retrieves trending narratives with volumeWeightedPrice performance, social author counts, market cap changes.
→
02 / SCORE
4-signal model
Each narrative scored 0–100 across rotation, momentum, volume, and social. Market regime filter applied to dampen scores in dangerous conditions.
→
03 / DECIDE
Conviction tiers
Score ≥70: full BUY (10% sizing). Score 55–70: reduced conviction BUY (5%). Score <40: SELL. Drawdown cap, stop-loss, gas reserve, and anti-churn guards applied. A forced minimum-activity trade (2.5%) fires only if 23+ hours pass with zero trades, required to qualify for live ranking.
→
04 / EXECUTE
Trust Wallet Agent Kit
Swaps via TWAK's non-custodial agent wallet — local signing, no raw private key handled by the agent. Verified BSC liquidity across a 12-token eligible universe: ETH, CAKE, DOGE, XRP, ADA, TWT, LINK, AVAX, FIL, LTC, INJ, UNI. Every tx logged to JSON.
↺
Scoring model

Four signals. One score.

No lagging indicators. Each signal captures a different dimension of narrative momentum.

35%
Rotation
volumeWeightedPrice vs. broader market. If a narrative outperforms BTC/ETH, capital is rotating in.
25%
Momentum
Market cap change blended 24h/7d/30d. Measures structural narrative growth, not just short-term noise.
20%
Volume
Trading activity surge as a liquidity confirmation. Real moves come with volume. Noise doesn't.
20%
Social
Unique CMC author count. Normalized relative to all narratives in each batch — not absolute numbers.
Regime filter
Dampens all scores in dangerous markets
